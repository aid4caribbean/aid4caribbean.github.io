About Aid for the Caribbean - A4C

Our Mission

At Aid for the Caribbean -A4C- we are dedicated to providing essential relief to vulnerable communities across the Caribbean region. Our mission focuses on addressing urgent needs such as healthcare, poverty alleviation, and education advancement.

Aid for the Caribbean: Making a Difference

Our work focuses on sending medical supplies to local hospitals and community non-governmental organisations in the Caribbean. We also donate mobility aids for older people, provide food and clothing to communities in need, and supply schools with educational materials.

Join Aid for the Caribbean's Fight Against poverty

Donate

Poverty is a serious issue in Caribbean countries, but you can help!  

We need medical supplies such as urinary bags, catheters, stoma bags, wound care products, syringes and similar. 

We also need mobility aids and assistive tools such as wheelchairs, walkers, commodes, nappies.  

Non-perishable food items, clothes, and equipment are also welcome.

Volunteer

More collection sites in different UK cities and volunteers to collect items, sort and transport to final storage are also needed.  We are also looking for volunteers traveling to the Caribbean who can bring an extra bag with donations.  

 Volunteering is a great way to make a difference.

Fundraising Campaigns

Help us to raise funds to send items to the Caribbean region! Every pound counts and makes a big difference.
